↓ What it’s like

Here’s what it’s like.

Try it — press play to hear a cue, or feel the nudge on your phone.

  • A quiet voice tells you what’s there. “Step ahead, two meters.” “Crosswalk on your right.” Your ears stay open. You still hear the world.
  • A gentle buzz at your wrist points the way. Both wrists at once means stop.
  • Most of the time it stays quiet, just watching. Ask — “What’s in front of me?” — and it takes a closer look.

Roana augments the white cane and guide dog — it doesn’t replace them, and it isn’t a medical device. (The voice you hear here is your browser’s, not Roana’s — just to give you the feel.)
